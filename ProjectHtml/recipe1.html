Khaman

Khaman is a traditional Indian dish made from lentils, chickpeas, and mixed vegetables. It is a sweet and savory dish that is often served with rice, dal, or mixed vegetable stir-fry. Khaman is made by boiling lentils in a mixture of water, chickpeas, and vegetables, and then simmering it until they are tender.

Preparation Time: 5 Minutes

Cooking Time: 10 Minutes

Ingredients for Batter:

Ingredients Quantity
Chickpea flour / Besan 2 cup
Cooking Soda 1 teaspoon
Sugar 1 teaspoon
salt 1/4 teaspoon
Ginger-green chilli paste 2 teaspoon
Turmeric powder small pinch

how to make khaman dhokla with step by step:

  1. firstly, in a large mixing bowl sieve 1½ cup besan and 3 tbsp rava.
  2. add ½ tsp ginger paste, 2 chilli, ¼ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp sugar, pinch hing, ½ tsp salt, 1 tbsp lemon juice and 1 tbsp oil.
  3. prepare a smooth batter adding 1 cup of water or as required.
  4. whisk for 5 minutes or until the batter turns smooth silky consistency.
  5. rest for 20 minutes, allowing besan to absorb water.
  6. additionally, add ½ tsp of eno fruit salt. you can alternatively use a pinch of baking soda.
  7. mix gently till the batter turns frothy
  8. immediately steam the dhokla batter for 20 minutes on medium flame or till the dhokla gets cooked completely.
  9. further, cut the dhokla to the desired shape
  10. prepare the tempering by heating 3 tsp oil. add in ½ tsp mustard, ½ tsp jeera, 1 tsp sesame seeds and pinch of hing.
  11. also add 2 green chilli, few curry leaves and allow to splutter.
  12. further add ¼ cup water, 1 tsp sugar and ¼ tsp salt.
  13. mix and boil the water well.add in 1 tsp lemon juice and mix well.
  14. pour the tempering over dhokla.garnish the dhokla with 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves.
  15. finally, serve instant khaman dhokla with green chutney and tamarind chutney.
