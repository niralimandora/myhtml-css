Dal-bhat

Gujarati Dal Bhat is a time-honored dish of sub-continent India prominent in every Gujarati kitchen for ages. This Gujarati staple dish is an integral part of an authentic Gujarati thali.

Preparation Time: 10 Minutes

Cooking Time: 20 Minutes

Ingredients for Batter:

Ingredients Quantity
split yellow pigeon peas / toor dal 1 cup
fresh tomatoes 2
fresh green chilli 1 teaspoon
ground coriander 1 teaspoon
salt 1/2 teaspoon
curry leaves 6 whole
Turmeric powder small pinch

how to make khaman dhokla with step by step:

  1. Soften the toor daal (split yellow pigeon peas)
  2. Add the spices.
  3. Boil on high heat
  4. Prepare the tadka (tempering)
  5. Add the tadka to the boiling dal (pigeon peas)
  6. finally, serve instant Dal with white rice.
